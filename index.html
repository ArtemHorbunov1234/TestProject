The golden time for patent attorneys seems to be long over. It starts from the population of technology and internet, whereby the hard work of patent attorneys was “disadvantaged”, as clients are access to profound information about prosecution proceedings on the internet and demand strong budget cut for works that were used to be billable for the patent attorney. Some of them take over some of the task internally so as to reduce the cost. The dilemmas here is well known: the budget from the clients is tidder and the personal cost of the paralegals are higher and higher. The profit margin drop dramatically! But we can turn this disadvantage into our advantage! With PatentMaker solutions based on AI technology, you can improve your efficiency not only for the work of the patent attorneys, but also greatly simply and shortly the workflows in the secretarial work and hence reduce the personal cost hereof.
